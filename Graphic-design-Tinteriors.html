Introduction

During my tenure as the Social Media and Marketing Head at T-Interiors, I was responsible for driving the company’s digital presence and branding efforts. Leading a team of four, I spearheaded the design and production of key branding materials, including logos, brochures, visiting cards, and social media posts. These materials were essential in establishing a cohesive brand identity and ensuring consistent messaging across all platforms.

In addition to overseeing design work, I played a crucial role in content creation by editing and designing video content for social media and advertisements. These videos were strategically crafted to engage our target audience, enhance brand visibility, and drive customer interaction. My responsibilities also included ensuring that our social media platforms were consistently updated with fresh, relevant content, keeping our audience engaged and informed.

Furthermore, I managed and optimized paid advertising campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, effectively increasing our reach and driving targeted traffic to our website and social media pages. These campaigns were instrumental in generating leads and boosting overall brand awareness.

What I learnt

In this role, I gained significant experience in leading a creative team and managing comprehensive marketing campaigns. I developed a deep understanding of how to create and maintain a strong brand presence across various digital platforms. The hands-on experience in video editing and content creation sharpened my skills in visual communication, while running successful ad campaigns on social media provided me with valuable insights into digital marketing strategies and analytics. This role also taught me the importance of adaptability and creativity in responding to the fast-paced demands of the marketing landscape. Overall, my time at T-Interiors was instrumental in refining my leadership abilities and expanding my expertise in social media and marketing.

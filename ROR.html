Rift of the Realms

Rift of the Realms delves into the timeless conflict between light and dark, good and evil. Players are thrust into a world where they must choose allegiance to either the radiant Light Realm or the ominous Dark Realm. The objective is to silence the opposing realm to ensure the survival of their own. To achieve this, players must breach the dimensional barrier, infiltrate enemy realm.

Checkout the Game Demo

Key Features

  • Multiplayer Mechanics: Implemented using Photon Unity Network (PUN) to ensure real-time player interactions and seamless synchronisation across all clients.
  • Dynamic Objectives: Each faction has distinct goals, requiring players to think strategically and adapt their plans as the game unfolds.
  • Player Movement & Combat: Players navigate the game world using WASD keys and can switch realms with the 'V' key. Real-time combat mechanics provide an engaging experience for players.
  • In-Game Economy: A dual-currency system rewards players with Silver and Gold Coins, allowing them to purchase items and enhancing competitive gameplay.

Game Mechanics and Objective System

  • Light Realm: Players must collect machine parts scattered across the map and repair machines before the timer runs out. This requires teamwork and coordination.
  • Dark Realm: This faction's players need to use strategic positioning and combat to thwart the Light Realm's efforts, creating a dynamic and tense gameplay experience.

What I learned

During the development of Rift of Realms, I had the opportunity to dive deep into the process of creating online multiplayer games using Unity and Photon PUN. This experience taught me how to manage real-time interactions effectively. I also worked on the backend, where I learned to integrate databases with XAMPP and the Laravel framework, ensuring our game ran smoothly. Additionally, I explored the use of VFX particles in Unity, which added a new level of visual appeal to the gameplay. Designing the game's UI allowed me to apply and deepen my understanding of Game UI principles, resulting in a user-friendly and visually engaging interface. Collaborating closely with a team of five was invaluable, as it strengthened my ability to work within a team environment, improving our communication and coordination throughout the project. This project was a comprehensive learning experience, helping me grow both technically and as a team player in game development.

Co-Founder & Technical Head, 8-Bit, Jain University – School of Sciences, Bangalore, India

June 2020 – May 2022

Introduction

During my undergraduate years at Jain University, I co-founded 8-Bit, a student society dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design and technology. As the Technical Head, I was deeply involved in transforming 8-Bit into a vibrant community where students could dive into their passions for gaming, graphic design, web development, video editing, and social media management. We built a collaborative environment that encouraged innovation and creative exploration. The following logo represents the Music Team of our university, and I had the opportunity to design it.

In my role, I orchestrated a variety of projects sourced from university forums and clubs, where I oversaw the technical aspects of these initiatives. This included designing backdrops and video advertisements for university events, ensuring that our work not only met the high standards expected but also enhanced the visual appeal of these events. Additionally, I was responsible for developing websites for interuniversity festivals, which involved coordinating with different teams to ensure that the websites were functional, user-friendly, and aligned with the event’s branding.

What I learned

Being the Technical Head of 8-Bit was a huge learning experience for me. I got to work on a variety of projects that pushed me to develop my skills in web development, video production, and project management. I also learned how to lead a team and manage multiple projects at once, making sure everything was completed on time and to a high standard. This experience really fueled my passion for technology and design and gave me a solid foundation in managing and executing creative projects.
